# Design Notes

- Mood: monochrome luxury, restrained, premium, automotive-inspired
- Palette: charcoal, black, silver, and soft white with very subtle contrast
- Typography: Inter for body and headings; keep labels understated and minimal
- Layout: spacious single-page landing page with a refined hero, clean service blocks, real photos, and a strong contact CTA
- Goal: make the site feel polished, high-end, and ready to publish immediately
