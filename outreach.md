# Outreach

## Initial contact
- **Opener:** Hi, I’m Tim — I took a quick look at Undefeated Landscape & Tree Service and put together a simple idea for how the site could turn more visitors into calls or texts for free estimates.
- **What to mention:**
  - Lead with free estimates and fast response.
  - Make tap-to-call and tap-to-text obvious.
  - Keep the main services plain and easy to scan on mobile.
- **Ask:** Offer a quick screenshot or a 2-minute walkthrough of how the homepage could convert better.
- **Tone:** Direct, local, no-pressure, short, practical, and specific.
