# Awesome Design Brief

This page should feel like a premium local-service landing page, not a generic template.

## Design goals
- Clean, cinematic, high-trust
- Strong mobile-first conversion flow
- Real photos up front
- Clear call-to-action above the fold
- Minimal clutter, no cheap stock look

## Visual direction
- Palette: deep navy / blue / green with subtle glow accents
- Typography: bold display headline + readable sans body
- Cards: soft borders, glassy surfaces, rounded corners
- Motion: tiny hover lift only, nothing flashy

## Layout recipe
1. Sticky header with phone CTA
2. Split hero with real image + quick facts
3. Service cards with simple scannable copy
4. Photo mosaic using public work photos
5. FAQ for clarity and SEO
6. Contact section with tap-to-call / text buttons

## Tone
- Confident
- Local
- Practical
- No fluff

## Guardrails
- Don’t invent reviews, licenses, or awards
- Don’t hide the phone number
- Don’t overload the page with borders or badges
- Keep the page fast and obvious on mobile
