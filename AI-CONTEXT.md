## Voice and copy rules
- Write like the company is speaking about itself using **we** / **our**
- Keep the tone direct and trustworthy
- Prefer plain service descriptions over hype
- Do not invent reviews, awards, licenses, guarantees, or certifications
- Do not claim work areas that are not publicly supported
- Do not make the copy sound scraped from social media or generic AI copy

## Design intent
- Premium but grounded local-service look
- Luxury / automotive-inspired styling without looking flashy
- Strong mobile-first layout
- Clear tap-to-call and tap-to-text actions
- Real photos over stock imagery
- High contrast, minimal clutter, easy scanning
- Subtle motion is okay if it feels polished

## Motion and UI notes
- The site uses a subtle animated background layer and scroll-linked motion
- Motion should stay elegant and low-key, not gimmicky
- If motion is reduced or disabled, the page should still feel complete and readable
- Footer links are intentionally kept visually hidden so the page does not feel cluttered
